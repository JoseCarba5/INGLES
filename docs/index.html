Most Important Soft Skills in IT Companies

Communication

Easy way to express ideas clearly and listen to others, which is very important in work teams and with clients.

Teamwork

Working well with others, sharing knowledge, and supporting common responsibilities.

Critical Thinking

Analyze problems logically and look for innovative and efficient solutions.

Adaptability

The ability to adjust to changing technology and project requirements quickly and effectively.

Proactivity

Anticipate problems and take initiative without being told all the time.
